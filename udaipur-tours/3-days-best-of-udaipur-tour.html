Tour Overview

Experience the absolute best that Udaipur has to offer. A comprehensive 3-day guided tour encompassing the royal architecture of City Palace, historical Jagdish Temple, vintage classic car museum, scenic ferry rides on Lake Pichola, Ambrai Ghat views, and day excursions to Eklingji and Nagda Hills.

Highlights

Explore City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Bagore-ki-Haveli, and Shilpgram.

Visit the Vintage collection of classic car museum.

Enjoy a scenic ferry ride on Lake Pichola.

Explore Eklingji Temple and Nagda Hills.

Day 01

Arrival at Udaipur

On the day of arriving at Udaipur Airport, our tour agent will meet you at the airport and then you have to move to your pre-booked hotel for night stay. After reaching the hotel, get relaxed or the remaining day is free for leisure. Spend overnight at the hotel. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

Day 02

Udaipur Sightseeing

On this day, after having morning breakfast, later move out for an all-day guided tour to the Udaipur city.
