Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-2

Delhi (Red Fort, Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb, India Gate).

Day 3

Mandawa (Shekhawati fresco havelis road trip).

Days 4-5

Bikaner to Jaisalmer (Fort, Patwon ki Haveli, camel dunes camp).

Days 6-7

Drive to Jodhpur (Mehrangarh Fort) then Ranakpur Jain temples.

Days 8-9

Udaipur (Lake Palace boat, City Palace, Shilpgram craft village).

Days 10-11

Drive to Jaipur (Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal).

Day 12

Drive via Fatehpur Sikri to Agra. Sunset Mehtab Bagh Taj view.

Day 13

Sunrise Taj Mahal visit; Agra Fort exploration.

Days 14-16

Train to Varanasi — Ganga Aarti, dawn boat ride, Sarnath, departure.
