Itinerary Highlights

Day 1

Arrive Delhi. Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk cycle-rickshaw adventure.

Day 2

Delhi to Agra. Mughal heritage at Agra Fort; free evening at leisure.

Day 3

Agra sunrise Taj Mahal visit, then drive via Fatehpur Sikri ghost city to Jaipur.

Day 4

Jaipur. Amber Fort elephant ride, City Palace, Jantar Mantar observatory.

Day 5

Jaipur. Hawa Mahal, local bazaar shopping, cultural puppet show evening.

Day 6

Jaipur to Delhi. Return drive and departure.
