Specially designed for first-time visitors to India with children. A perfectly paced itinerary taking families across Rajasthan's royal forts, sand dunes, and lake palaces — ending at the iconic Taj Mahal.

Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-2

Delhi arrival and orientation sightseeing.

Day 3

Drive to Mandawa — painted havelis and vibrant street art.

Days 4-5

Bikaner (Junagarh Fort) then Jaisalmer — camel safari, desert camp.

Day 6

Jaisalmer to Jodhpur (Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan Palace).

Day 7

Drive via Ranakpur Jain temples to Mount Abu — hill station relaxation.

Days 8-9

Udaipur (Lake Pichola boat, City Palace, Jagdish Temple).

Day 10

Chittorgarh Fort, drive to Ajmer/Pushkar.

Day 11

Pushkar (holy lake, Brahma temple, camel fair if in season).

Days 12-13

Jaipur (Amber Fort elephant, Hawa Mahal, bazaars).

Days 14-15

Drive to Agra. Sunrise Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, departure.
