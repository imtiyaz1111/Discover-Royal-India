Bikaner (Junagarh Fort, camel research centre) then Jaisalmer.

Jaisalmer (Fort, Patwon ki Haveli, camel safari, desert camp night).

Days 10-11

Drive to Jodhpur (Mehrangarh Fort, spice market, blue city walk) then Ranakpur Jain temples.

Days 12-13

Udaipur (Lake Pichola boat ride, City Palace, Saheliyon ki Bari) then fly to Mumbai.

Day 14

Mumbai city tour (Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Elephanta Caves) and departure.
