Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-2

Delhi (Old Delhi cycle-rickshaw, India Gate, Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb).

Days 3-4

Jaipur (Amber Fort elephant ride, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal).

Day 5

Drive Jaipur to Agra via Fatehpur Sikri. Sunrise Taj Mahal.

Day 6

Drive Agra to Orchha. Medieval cenotaphs, river walk.

Day 7

Drive to Khajuraho. Temple complex light and sound show.

Days 8-9

Khajuraho to Varanasi. Morning boat ride on the Ganges, Sarnath Buddhist site, departure.
