Tour Overview

Experience the classic short loop of the Golden Triangle. Witness the red sandstone monuments of Delhi, the romance of the Taj Mahal in Agra, and the glorious forts of Jaipur in a perfect 4-day package.

Itinerary Highlights

Day 1

Delhi Arrival & Sightseeing (Explore Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb, and India Gate)

Day 2

Delhi to Agra (Drive to Agra, visit Agra Fort and witness the Taj Mahal at sunset)

Day 3

Agra to Jaipur (Drive to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri, explore City Palace and local bazaars)

Day 4

Jaipur to Delhi & Departure (Visit Amber Fort in the morning, then drive back to Delhi)
