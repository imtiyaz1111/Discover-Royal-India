Tour Overview

An absolute feast of colors, sights, and heritage. Marvel at the Taj Mahal, Jaipur's bazaar, Pushkar's lake, and Udaipur's palace.

Highlights

Explore historic sites of Old Delhi and New Delhi (Jama Masjid, Lotus Temple)

Experience Gatiman Express premium train ride to Agra

Witness sunrise at Taj Mahal & tour red sandstone Agra Fort

Tour pink city Jaipur, Amber Fort palace, Hawa Mahal and Jantar Mantar observatory

Visit the ruined Mughal capital city of Fatehpur Sikri

Visit Lord Brahma Temple and the holy lake ghats of Pushkar

Itinerary

Day 01

Delhi

Delhi: Delhi is the capital of India and its third largest city. Delhi, the seat of one of the world’s largest democracies is a combination of “Old Delhi”- the capital of Muslim India and “New Delhi” the imperial city created as the nation’s capital by the British. There’s a unique blend of tradition and modernity. Here one can see mingling of many cultures, languages, traditions and people in complete harmony.

Day 02

Delhi

After breakfast Proceed For Old Delhi Sightseeing Tour. Visit Raj Ghat (The cremation spot of Mahatma Gandhi), Jama Masjid (the largest Friday congregational mosque in India), Qutub Minar (the tallest stone tower in India), Lotus Temple (Bahai House of Worship), India Gate, President House and Parliament House. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 03

Delhi - Agra (By Train)

After breakfast transfer to Railway station to board Gatiman Express train to Agra (DEP: 08:10 HRS, ARR: 09:45 HRS). On arrival, transfer to hotel. Agra's resplendent history and the exquisite Taj Mahal will captivate your experience. Spend your night in the town of the fabled Taj Mahal. Overnight stay in Agra.

Enquire Now
Day 04

Agra – Fatehpur Sikri – Jaipur

Sunrise visit to the Taj Mahal. Return to hotel for breakfast. Visit Agra Fort, built by Emperor Akbar between 1565 and 1573. Visit Itmad-ud-Daulah, also known as the Baby Taj. Drive to Jaipur, visiting the ruined Mughal capital city Fatehpur Sikri (UNESCO World Heritage Site) en-route. Continue to Jaipur, the Pink City. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 05

Jaipur

Proceed for sightseeing tour of Jaipur. Visit Amber Fort Palace (classic romantic fort built in Hindu-Mughal style), City Palace, Jantar Mantar (the largest stone observatory), and Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). Spend the afternoon free for shopping gold, silver jewellery, and carpets. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 06

Jaipur – Pushkar

After breakfast drive to Pushkar. Visit Brahma Temple (the only temple dedicated to Lord Brahma) and walk around the holy Pushkar Lake, surrounded by 52 bathing ghats. Overnight stay in Pushkar.

Day 07

Pushkar – Udaipur

After breakfast drive to Udaipur, the lake city set around artificial lakes. Check in to the hotel and enjoy the views of Lake Pichola. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

Day 08

Udaipur

Enjoy a guided sightseeing tour of Udaipur. Visit City Palace museum, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon Ki Bari, Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir (Folk art museum), and Pratap Memorial. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

Day 09

Udaipur

Free and easy day to relax or explore Udaipur's local art and lake views independently. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

Day 10

Udaipur – Delhi (By Flight)

After breakfast, transfer to Udaipur airport for your connect flight to Delhi (INDIGO DEP: 13:15 HRS, ARR: 14:35 HRS). Connect further to your flight home with sweet memories of the Colourful Rajasthan Tour.
