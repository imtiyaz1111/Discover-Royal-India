Tour Overview

Combine the absolute best of North India's historic monuments, cultural heritage, and spiritual landmarks with the breathtaking natural beauty and rich spiritual culture of Nepal. Explore the Golden Triangle of Delhi, Jaipur, and Agra, delve into the erotic carvings of Khajuraho, feel the sacred energy of Varanasi, and journey onwards to Kathmandu, Chitwan, and Pokhara in Nepal.

Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-3: Delhi & Jaipur - Forts & Culture

Discover Delhi's monuments and drive to Jaipur to explore the Amber Fort and Palace of Winds.

Days 4-5: Fatehpur Sikri & Agra - The Epitome of Love

Visit the abandoned Mughal city of Fatehpur Sikri and marvel at the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort.

Days 6-7: Orchha & Khajuraho - Architecture & Temples

Journey through the medieval town of Orchha and see the stunning UNESCO World Heritage temples of Khajuraho.

Days 8-9: Varanasi - Spiritual Heart of India

Experience the magical Ganga Aarti ceremony and a sunrise boat ride on the holy Ganges River.

Days 10-12: Kathmandu & Chitwan - Nepal's Heritage & Wildlife

Fly to Kathmandu to visit ancient shrines, then drive to Chitwan National Park for a wildlife jungle safari.

Days 13-15: Pokhara & Kathmandu - Himalayas & Departure

Rejoice in the stunning mountain views of Annapurna in Pokhara before heading back to Kathmandu for departure.
