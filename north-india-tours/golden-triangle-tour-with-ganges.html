Tour Overview

India's most iconic introductory circuit (Delhi, Jaipur, and Agra) coupled with the sacred vibes of Varanasi and the Ganges River. Designed to showcase key historical architecture and India's ancient spirituality in a perfect 9-day itinerary with expert guides and premium, comfortable transport.

Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-2: Delhi - Old & New Heritage

Tour the heritage sites of Old Delhi and New Delhi's modern government spaces.

Days 3-4: Jaipur - Palace & Pink City

Visit Amber Fort, enjoy a photo-op at Jal Mahal, and walk the colorful bazaars of Jaipur.

Day 5: Fatehpur Sikri to Agra

Drive to Agra via the ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri. Visit the Agra Fort.

Day 6: Taj Mahal & Train to Orchha / Khajuraho

Watch the Taj Mahal glow at sunrise before taking a train to Orchha and Khajuraho.

Days 7-9: Varanasi to Delhi - Divine Ganges

Experience the ultimate spiritual energy of Varanasi before flying back to Delhi to conclude the tour.
