Tour Overview

Embark on an unforgettable bespoke journey through the royal state of Rajasthan and the iconic landmarks of North India. This hand-crafted tour is designed for travelers who appreciate history, heritage architecture, and the timeless beauty of majestic palaces and Havelis. From the bustling streets of Delhi to the romantic desert fortresses of Jaisalmer and Jodhpur, and the iconic Taj Mahal, experience royalty at its finest.

Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-2: Delhi - Capital Gateway

Explore the historic landmarks of Old and New Delhi, including the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb.

Days 3-4: Mandawa & Bikaner - Land of Painted Havelis

Visit the open-air art gallery of Mandawa and the grand Junagarh Fort in Bikaner.

Days 5-6: Jaisalmer - Golden Sandstone Fort

Witness the beautiful Thar Desert, explore the living Jaisalmer Fort, and enjoy a scenic sunset camel safari.

Days 7-8: Jodhpur & Udaipur - Forts & Lake Palaces

Marvel at the Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur, and the romantic lake city of Udaipur with its stunning City Palace.

Days 9-11: Pushkar & Jaipur - Pink City & Sacred Lakes

Visit the sacred Brahma Temple in Pushkar, and the majestic Amber Fort and Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.

Days 12-15: Agra & Delhi - The Taj Mahal

Experience the mesmerizing Taj Mahal at sunrise before concluding your royal journey in Delhi.
