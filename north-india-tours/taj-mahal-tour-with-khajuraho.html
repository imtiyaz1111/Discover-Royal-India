Tour Overview

Experience the ultimate short journey through the architectural wonders of North India. This 7-day tour takes you through the beautiful landmarks of Delhi, Jaipur, and Agra, and onwards to the iconic temples of Khajuraho (celebrated for their intricate medieval stone sculptures) and the spiritual capital Varanasi.

Itinerary Highlights

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

Meet and greet at Delhi airport, followed by check-in at a luxury boutique hotel.

Day 2: Delhi to Jaipur

Drive to Jaipur. Explore the grand Amber Fort, City Palace, and local astronomical observatories.

Day 3: Jaipur to Agra

Travel to Agra. Enjoy a sunset visit to the breathtaking, iconic Taj Mahal.

Day 4: Agra to Khajuraho

Take a train or flight to Khajuraho and tour the world-famous Western Group of temples.

Day 5: Khajuraho to Varanasi

Travel to Varanasi and witness the mesmerizing Evening Ganga Aarti ceremony.

Days 6-7: Varanasi to Delhi

Enjoy a sunrise boat cruise on the Ganges before taking your return flight to Delhi.
