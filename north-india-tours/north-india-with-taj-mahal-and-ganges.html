Tour Overview

Immerse yourself in the cultural essence and deep spirituality of North India. This comprehensive tour connects the magnificent, romantic Taj Mahal in Agra with the historic fortresses of Rajasthan and the sacred, timeless city of Varanasi on the banks of the Ganges. Watch sunrise rituals, explore ancient alleyways, and enjoy unmatched luxury hotels.

Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-3: Delhi - Capital Landmarks

Tour India's capital city, exploring Old and New Delhi's best architectural sites.

Days 4-7: Bikaner & Jaisalmer - Desert Forts

Explore the stunning palaces of Mandawa, the desert citadel of Jaisalmer, and Bikaner's fort.

Days 8-10: Jodhpur & Udaipur - Royal Lakes

See Jodhpur's blue city and Mehrangarh Fort, then travel to Udaipur's lake palace region.

Days 11-13: Jaipur & Agra - Golden Triangle

Explore the Amber Fort in Jaipur and witness the magnificent Taj Mahal in Agra.

Days 14-16: Varanasi to Delhi - Sacred Ganges

Experience the divine Ganges River in Varanasi before traveling back to Delhi for departure.
