Tour Overview

Embark on the ultimate Indian wildlife and heritage adventure. This comprehensive 16-day tour features the iconic Taj Mahal, the pink city of Jaipur, and the best tiger-spotting national parks: Ranthambore, Bandhavgarh, Kanha, and Pench. Witness breathtaking architecture, stunning forest landscapes, and rich wildlife ecosystems.

Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-3: Delhi & Ranthambore - City to Jungle

Sightsee in Delhi, then board a train to Ranthambore National Park for your first thrilling safaris.

Days 4-6: Bharatpur, Fatehpur Sikri & Agra

Explore the famous Keoladeo Bird Sanctuary in Bharatpur, the abandoned Mughal city of Fatehpur Sikri, and the legendary Taj Mahal.

Days 7-9: Khajuraho & Bandhavgarh National Park

Tour Khajuraho's medieval temples, then head to Bandhavgarh for incredible tiger tracking.

Days 10-12: Kanha National Park - Deep Wilderness

Enjoy multiple jeep safaris in the vast meadows and dense sal forests of Kanha Tiger Reserve.

Days 13-16: Pench, Nagpur & Mumbai - Departure

Spot wildlife in Pench (setting of The Jungle Book), then fly to Mumbai via Nagpur to catch your onward flight home.
