Tour Overview

Combine the absolute best of cultural sightseeing with thrilling jungle safaris. This tour covers historical monuments in Delhi, Jaipur, and Agra, and then leads you to India's most famous wildlife national parks. With expert naturalists, seek the elusive royal Bengal tiger in Ranthambore, Bandhavgarh, and Kanha National Parks, enjoying luxurious wilderness lodges along the way.

Itinerary Highlights

Days 1-2: Delhi - Sightseeing

Discover old monuments and landmarks in Delhi before your wildlife and cultural journey begins.

Days 3-5: Jaipur & Ranthambore - Heritage & Tigers

See Amber Fort and Hawa Mahal in Jaipur, then enjoy thrilling safaris at Ranthambore National Park.

Days 6-7: Fatehpur Sikri & Agra - The Taj Mahal

Visit Agra's Taj Mahal and Red Fort to round out the historic Golden Triangle section.

Days 8-10: Bandhavgarh - High Density Tiger Haven

Journey to Bandhavgarh National Park for multiple morning and afternoon jeep safaris.

Days 11-13: Kanha National Park - Kipling's Jungle

Explore the lush sal forests of Kanha, the inspiration behind Rudyard Kipling's Jungle Book.

Days 14-15: Nagpur to Delhi - Departure

Fly from Nagpur to Delhi for your return flight home.
