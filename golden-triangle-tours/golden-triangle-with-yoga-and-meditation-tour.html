Tour Overview

Balance the sightseeing of the Golden Triangle with a peaceful wellness retreat in Rishikesh, the world capital of Yoga.

Highlights

Visit to wonderful Taj Mahal & Tonga Ride in Agra

Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Jaipur

Explore the magnificent Forts & enjoy Elephant Ride in Jaipur

Visit Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Birla Temple and Qutub Minar, Delhi

Enjoy Rickshaw Ride in Old Delhi & Train Journey to Haridwar

Visit Har Ki Pauri and witness traditional Ganga Aarti in Haridwar & Rishikesh

Visit Laxman Jhula, Triveni Ghat and enjoy River Rafting, Rishikesh

Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival at Delhi

Arrive Delhi’s Indira Gandhi International Airport in the evening. You will be met at the airport by a Discover Royal India representative who will give you a warm welcome by fresh flower garlands to transfer you to the hotel by a deluxe air-conditioned Car/Coach. On arrival check in & free evening for relax. Overnight at hotel.

Enquire Now
Day 02

Delhi Sightseeing

Morning, after breakfast we leave for a sightseeing tour of Old & New Delhi. In Old Delhi we will visit the largest mosque in India called Jama Masjid. The highlight of the tour is an exhilarating rickshaw ride at Old Delhi through the bustling and colourful streets of fabled shopping bazaar, Chandni Chowk (Silver Street). We drive pass to Red Fort and reach Rajghat, a memorial to Mahatma Gandhi. In New Delhi we will visit Qutub Minar Tower, an UNESCO World Heritage Site. The India Gate, President's House, Parliament House, and Humayun's Tomb. Overnight stay at Delhi.

Day 03

Delhi - Jaipur (By Road approx 280 Kms / 5 Hrs)

Morning, we will travel to the royal city of Jaipur, also known as 'The Pink City'. On arrival check in to your hotel. Evening, we will visit the Birla Temple also known as Laxmi Narayan Mandir and witness an Aarti ceremony. Later, for dinner time, visit the unique ethnic resort Chokhi Dhani to enjoy puppet shows, folk dances, and local thali meal. Overnight at Jaipur Hotel.

Enquire Now
Day 04

Jaipur Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit Amber Fort, where you will enjoy an Elephant Ride to the top of the palace. Afternoon, continue your journey in the Jaipur city tour including City Palace, Jantar Mantar (UNESCO stone observatory), and a brief photography stop at Hawa Mahal. In the evening, explore the city markets for precious stones, silver jewelry, paintings, or hand-printed textiles. Overnight at Jaipur hotel.

Day 05

Jaipur - Fatehpur Sikri - Agra (By Road approx 250 Kms / 5 Hrs)

After breakfast, we will take you on a route to Agra. Enroute we will visit Fatehpur Sikri – an UNESCO World Heritage Site which has been described as "the world’s most perfectly preserved ghost town". Overnight at hotel in Agra.

Day 06

Agra Sightseeing - Delhi

After breakfast, explore the monumental heritage of Agra, including the magnificent Taj Mahal (Closed on Fridays). Followed by lunch, visit the imposing Agra Fort and the Tomb of Itimad-Ud-Daulah. Later back to Delhi and overnight stay at hotel.

Enquire Now
Day 07

Delhi - Haridwar (By Road approx 220 Kms / 5 Hrs)

After breakfast, we will drive towards Haridwar. One of the first towns where Ganga emerges from the mountains to touch the plains. Later visit Haridwar Ghats to look at the breathtakingly beautiful Ganga Aarti in the evening. Overnight at Haridwar.

Day 08

Haridwar - Rishikesh (By Road approx 21 Kms / 1 Hrs)

Post breakfast, drive to the world capital of Yoga Rishikesh. Once arrived, check in to the Yoga Ashram. Visit the Triveni Ghat to witness the evening Aarti. Also visit Rishikund, Lakshman Jhula, and Geeta Bhawan. Overnight at Rishikesh Ashram.

Day 09

Rishikesh - Yoga Session (Day 1)

Early morning walk to the yoga centre. This day we will do relaxation, Asana, followed by Yoga Nidra. Learn about breathing awareness and stretching exercises. Evening yoga & traditional meditation class with a guest instructor. Overnight stay at Ashram.

Enquire Now
Day 10

Rishikesh - Yoga Session (Day 2)

This morning get training for PRANAVAM (advance breathing) and Dynamic Yoga. After breakfast drive up to Vashisht Gufa, where the saint Vasistha meditated, a perfect place for transcendental meditation. Late afternoon drive back to Ashram. Evening meditation & lecture. Overnight at Rishikesh.

Day 11

Rishikesh - Yoga Session (Day 3)

Full Day enjoy PRANAYAM YOGA & Advanced Techniques of Yoga. Overnight at Rishikesh.

Day 12

Rishikesh - Yoga Session (Day 4)

Early morning enjoy a VIPASSANA class. Evening Curative Yoga & Meditation class. Overnight at Rishikesh.

Day 13

Train From Haridwar - Delhi (Departure)

Early morning transfer to Haridwar railway station to board train to Delhi @ 06:30/11:15 hrs. On arrival in Delhi transfer to International airport to board flight for onward destination.
