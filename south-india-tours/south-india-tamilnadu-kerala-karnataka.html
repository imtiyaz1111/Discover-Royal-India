Tour Overview

A grand temple and landscape tour across three vibrant southern states. Marvel at giant Dravidian temples and historic monuments.

Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival in Chennai

Upon arrival at Chennai International Airport, complete immigration and customs formalities and meet our representative. You will be transferred to your hotel for check-in. After a long journey, the rest of the day is kept free to relax. In the evening, you may visit Marina Beach, one of the longest city beaches in the world. Overnight stay in Chennai.

Enquire Now
Day 02

Chennai Sightseeing

After breakfast, proceed for a full-day city tour of Chennai. Visit Kapaleeshwarar Temple, a fine example of Dravidian architecture. Continue to Fort St. George, the first British fortress in India. Later, explore the Government Museum and San Thome Basilica, built over the tomb of St. Thomas the Apostle. Return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 03

Chennai - Mahabalipuram

Post breakfast, drive to Mahabalipuram along the scenic East Coast Road. On arrival, check in at the hotel. Later, visit the Shore Temple overlooking the Bay of Bengal, the Five Rathas carved from single stones, Arjuna’s Penance with its massive rock relief, Krishna’s Butter Ball, and Tiger Cave. Evening at leisure. Overnight in Mahabalipuram.

Enquire Now
Day 04

Mahabalipuram – Kanchipuram – Mahabalipuram

Today, enjoy an excursion to Kanchipuram, one of the seven sacred cities of India. Visit famous temples such as Ekambareswarar Temple, Kailasanathar Temple, and Kamakshi Amman Temple. You will also see traditional silk weaving centers that produce the famous Kanchipuram sarees. Return to Mahabalipuram in the evening. Overnight stay.

Day 05

Mahabalipuram – Tanjore

After an early breakfast, drive to Tanjore. En route, you may stop at Pondicherry to experience its French colonial charm. On arrival in Tanjore, visit the magnificent Brihadeeswarar Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Later, explore Tanjore Palace and the Art Gallery showcasing Chola bronzes and murals. Overnight in Tanjore.

Enquire Now
Day 06

Tanjore – Trichy – Madurai

After breakfast, depart for Madurai, stopping at Trichy. Visit the Srirangam Ranganathaswamy Temple, one of the largest functioning temple complexes in the world, and Rock Fort Temple offering panoramic city views. Continue your drive to Madurai. Check in at the hotel. Overnight stay in Madurai.

Day 07

Madurai Sightseeing

Begin the day with a visit to the renowned Meenakshi Amman Temple, famous for its towering gopurams and intricate sculptures. Later, visit Thirumalai Nayakar Palace and Gandhi Memorial Museum. Walk through the colorful local markets in the evening. Optional cultural performance. Overnight in Madurai.

Enquire Now
Day 08

Madurai – Thekkady

After breakfast, drive to Thekkady in Kerala. On arrival, check in to a resort near Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. In the afternoon, enjoy a boat ride on Periyar Lake, where wildlife such as elephants and deer can often be spotted along the forest banks. Evening free or optional cultural shows. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 09

Thekkady – Kumarakom

Post breakfast, drive to Kumarakom, situated on the banks of Vembanad Lake. Check in to your lakeside resort. The rest of the day is free for relaxation or optional visits to Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary. Enjoy the peaceful surroundings. Overnight stay in Kumarakom.

Enquire Now
Day 10

Kumarakom – Alleppey (Houseboat)

After breakfast, proceed to Alleppey. Board a traditional houseboat and begin your backwater cruise through narrow canals, palm-lined shores, and village life. All meals will be served on board. Relax and enjoy the scenic beauty. Overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 11

Alleppey – Cochin

After breakfast on the houseboat, disembark and drive to Cochin. Visit the Dutch Palace, Jewish Synagogue, St. Francis Church, and see the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets. In the evening, you may watch a Kathakali dance performance. Overnight in Cochin.

Enquire Now
Day 12

Cochin – Ooty

After breakfast, drive to Ooty through scenic mountain roads, forests, and tea plantations. On arrival, check in to the hotel and relax after the long journey. Evening free at leisure. Overnight stay in Ooty.

Day 13

Ooty Sightseeing

Enjoy sightseeing in Ooty, visiting the Botanical Gardens, Ooty Lake, Rose Garden, and Doddabetta Peak, the highest point in the Nilgiris. Optional toy train ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway. Overnight in Ooty.

Enquire Now
Day 14

Ooty – Mysore

After breakfast, drive to Mysore, stopping at Pykara Falls and scenic viewpoints along the way. On arrival, explore the bustling Devaraja Market. Check in to the hotel. Overnight stay in Mysore.

Day 15

Mysore Sightseeing

Visit the majestic Mysore Palace, followed by St. Philomena’s Church and Chamundi Hill Temple. In the evening, visit Brindavan Gardens to witness the musical fountain show. Overnight in Mysore.

Enquire Now
Day 16

Mysore – Bandipur

Drive to Bandipur National Park. Check in to a jungle lodge. Later, enjoy a safari through the forest, offering chances to spot wildlife such as elephants, deer, and birds. Overnight stay in Bandipur.

Day 17

Bandipur – Bangalore

After breakfast, drive to Bangalore. On arrival, visit Lalbagh Botanical Garden or Cubbon Park. Later, enjoy shopping or leisure time in the city. Overnight stay in Bangalore.

Enquire Now
Day 18

Bangalore Sightseeing

Proceed for sightseeing in Bangalore, covering Bangalore Palace, Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace, Bull Temple, and Vidhana Soudha. Evening free at leisure. Overnight in Bangalore.

Day 19

Departure from Bangalore

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Bangalore Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey.
