Tour Overview

Experience the highlights of Kerala's famous waterways, spice valleys, and beach resorts in a beautifully paced 9-day itinerary.

Highlights

Explore vibrant, multicultural Cochin with its historic charm.

Wander through lush tea plantations, visit a tea museum and enjoy hill-station vibes in Munnar.

Experience wildlife sanctuaries, spice gardens and a boat ride in forest-fringed Thekkady.

Relax by lakes and bird-sanctuary zones in Kumarakom.

Cruise the tranquil backwaters and enjoy a unique overnight stay on a houseboat in Alleppey.

Unwind on golden beaches and enjoy coastal retreat at Kovalam.

End your journey with the calm and culture of Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum), blending history, coastal atmosphere and local tradition.

Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival & Cochin Sightseeing

Arrive in Cochin. Meet your guide/representative and check-in to the hotel. Spend the day exploring — visit historic spots like the old town, local heritage sites and enjoy the multicultural vibe of the city. Overnight stay in Cochin.

Day 02

Cochin - Munnar (125 Kms/4 hrs approx.)

After breakfast, travel to Munnar. Check-in at your stay in Munnar and relax amidst the hills. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 03

Explore Munnar

In the morning: relax or take in the scenic calm. Later: sightseeing — explore tea plantations, visit the Tea Museum, and enjoy views of hills and valleys. Optionally visit natural landmarks around Munnar. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Enquire Now
Day 04

Munnar - Thekkady (90 Kms/ 3 hrs approx.)

Depart after breakfast for Thekkady. On arrival, visit wildlife reserve/spice plantations. Go for a boat ride on the lake inside the sanctuary and immerse in Kerala’s forest-and-spice ambience. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 05

Thekkady - Kumarakom ( 122 km / 3.5 hrs approx.)

After breakfast, drive to Kumarakom. Check-in at accommodation near backwaters/lakes. Later visit bird sanctuary or enjoy a serene riverside/lakeside setting. Overnight stay in Kumarakom.

Enquire Now
Day 06

Kumarakom — Alleppey (32 Kms/ 1 hrs approx)

Post breakfast, head to Alleppey and board a private traditional houseboat. Cruise through the peaceful backwaters of Kerala, watch palm-lined canals, villages, water-life. Dinner and overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 07

Alleppey – Kovalam (160Kms/5.5hrs Approx)

After disembarking from houseboat and breakfast, drive to Kovalam. Check into beach-side hotel or resort. The rest of the day is leisure: enjoy beach walk, sunset, chill by the sea. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Enquire Now
Day 08

Relax / Leisure at Kovalam

Full day at leisure. You may enjoy Ayurvedic massage, beach-side rest, sunbathing, or just soak in the coastal vibe and local seafood. Overnight stay at Kovalam.

Day 09

Kovalam — Trivandrum (15 kms/ 30 mins approx.)

After breakfast, transfer to Trivandrum. Trip ends here.
