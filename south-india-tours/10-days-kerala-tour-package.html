Tour Overview

Spend 10 glorious days exploring the best hill stations, spice forests, and tranquil lakes of Kerala and neighboring Tamil Nadu.

Highlights

Enjoy city tours of Kochi & experience giant Chinese Fishing Nets

Explore the Fascinating Nilgiri Mountains & scenic Munnar tea gardens

Explore Periyar National Park by jeep safari and boat ride

Visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary & Bay Island Driftwood Museum

Rejuvenate the body and mind with an Ayurveda massage

Itinerary

Day 01

Arrive Cochin

Arrive in Cochin – one of the finest harbors on the Arabian coastline. Upon landing, transfer to your hotel to relax and settle in. Overnight stay in Cochin.

Enquire Now
Day 02

Cochin

After breakfast start the visit of Cochin (Kochi) covering the famous Mattancherry Palace, St. Francis Church, and the Jewish Synagogue. Enjoy the spectacular view of giant Chinese Fishing Nets being lowered on the famous Vasco-da-Gama Square. In the evening watch the Kathakali dance performance by trained dancers. Overnight stay in Cochin.

Day 03

Cochin to Munnar

After breakfast depart by road to Munnar – an idyllic tourist destination offering an unending expanse of tea plantation. The presence of lush green surroundings, lakes, reservoirs, and forests are there to enthral you. On arrival at Munnar, check-in to your hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. You can explore the enchanting beauty of this beautiful hill station during the evening hours and enjoy the sunset. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Enquire Now
Day 04

Munnar

After breakfast, proceed for your sightseeing tour of Munnar. Begin with Atukkad Waterfalls, Chithirapuram, Blossom Park, and Pothamedu View Point. Later, enjoy an excursion to Eravikulam National Park, where one can spot the endangered species of Nilgiri Tahr. Spend the evening exploring the Rajamala Hills, before returning to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 05

Munnar to Kodaikanal

After breakfast proceed to Kodaikanal, The land where green mountains lie around the corner. Any nature lover can sit and enjoy the beauty of this falls for hours. On arrival, check in at hotel. Rest of the day free in the lap of the nature. Overnight stay in Kodaikanal.

Enquire Now
Day 06

Kodaikanal

Today we spent our day visiting Berijam lake, Bryant Park, Green Valley View, Shenbagnur Museum, and Silent Valley View. Overnight stay in Kodaikanal.

Day 07

Kodaikanal to Thekkady (Periyar)

Early breakfast departs by road to Thekkady. Enjoy the drive as the road winds through the thick jungles and rich plantations. On arrival, check in to your hotel and get ready for your afternoon jeep safari at the PeriyarNational Park. Periyar is a true paradise of elephants. Later in the evening, enjoy a boat ride at Periyar Lake. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Enquire Now
Day 08

Thekkady to Kumarakom

After breakfast, depart by road to Kumarakom – a paradise for bird watchers. On reaching Kumarakom, visit the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary. After bird watching head to your hotel for check-in. After resting for a while, visit the Bay Island Driftwood Museum. Kumarakom village is situated on the banks of Vembanad Lake, which can be visited in the evening for sightseeing. Overnight stay in Kumarakom.

Day 09

Kumarakom

Breakfast at Hotel. Day at leisure to explore the surroundings, Vembanad lake views, and local village tracks. Overnight stay in Kumarakom.

Enquire Now
Day 10

Kumarakom to Cochin Airport to Back Home

Breakfast at Hotel and on time transfer to Cochin Airport to board the flight for home country.
